About Me

Adams graduated from the College of Charleston in 2011 with a Bachelor of Science in International Business. Adams commissioned as an officer in the U.S. Marine Corps immeadiatly upon graduation. Adams served as a Military Police Officer, leading a platoon of 70 Marines for three years. During his fourth year, Adams was selected to join the Marine Corps Information Operations Center in Quantico, VA as an Information Operations Officer and Psychological Operations Officer. Durning this time, Adams developed and led multiple goverment public relations and cyber programs spanning multiple countries. Adams ended his service in the Marine Corps as a Captain in 2017. In June of 2018, Adams completed his Project Management Professional (PMP) certification with the Project Management Institute as well as the Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Course with the Six Sigma Global Institute. In October 2018, Adams graduatied from the Computer Coding Boot Camp at the University of Texas at Austin.
